🌱 About Kisan Market

Kisan Market is a platform dedicated to empowering farmers and delivering fresh, high-quality agricultural products directly to consumers. We believe in fair trade, sustainability, and creating a system that benefits both farmers and buyers.

By eliminating unnecessary middlemen, we ensure farmers receive fair prices and customers get farm-fresh products at the best rates.
