Product Management Guide

Welcome to the product management guide for your Kisan eCommerce dashboard. This page will help you understand how to manage your products effectively on the platform.

Step 1: Add New Products

To add a new product to your shop, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Add Product section in your dashboard.
  2. Fill in the product details including name, description, price, and category.
  3. Upload an image of the product for better visibility.
  4. Click the Add Product button to save your product.
Go to Add Product

Step 2: Manage Your Products

Once your products are added, you can manage them from your product list:

  1. Navigate to the Manage Products section.
  2. Here, you will see a list of all the products you have uploaded.
  3. You can edit or delete any product by clicking the respective option next to each product.
Go to Manage Products

Step 3: View Product Details

If you want to see more details about any specific product:

  1. Click on the product name in the product list.
  2. You will be taken to the Product Details page where you can view all the details of the product.
View Product Example

Additional Tips

  • Make sure to upload high-quality images for your products to attract more customers.
  • Update the product description regularly to reflect accurate and detailed information.
  • Track your inventory and adjust the quantity to avoid selling out of stock products.
