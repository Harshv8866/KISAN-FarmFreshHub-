❓ Help & Support

Need assistance? We’re here to guide you through every step.

📦 How to Place an Order

  • Browse the homepage or product categories.
  • Click on a product to view its details and add it to your cart.
  • Go to your cart and click “Checkout”.
  • Enter your shipping details and complete payment.

💳 Payments

  • We support Razorpay for secure online payments (UPI, cards, etc.).
  • If your payment fails, check your internet connection or contact your bank.

🚚 Shipping & Delivery

  • Delivery is available within 2–5 business days.
  • You can track your orders from the “Orders” section.

👤 Account Help

  • Update your profile and picture in your dashboard.
  • Use “Forgot Password” on the login page to reset your password.

📞 Contact Us

If you still need help, email us at support@kisaneco.com or call us at 📱 +91-9876543210.
